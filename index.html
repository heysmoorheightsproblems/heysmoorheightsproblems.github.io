Heysmoor Heights Problems

This website is for anyone considering moving into Heysmoor Heights, It was made by the residents.

This is what your Landlord or Letting agent will tell you.

These are luxury apartments situated on the outskirts of Liverpool city centre. The property is in an excellent location with easy access to public transport to all parts of the city and local shops and amenities within 5 and 15 minutes walking distance. You also have 2 fantastic parks, Sefton Park and Princes Park which host a variety of events through the year.

This is what you really need to know, as this is what your letting agent will not tell you.

  1. Waste bins are not emptied on a regular basis usually between 2 to 4 weeks between collections, it gets to the point of the bins being full and bin bags being stacked up on the floor.

  2. Security Gate Leading onto croxteth Road is always open. Anyone can walk in!

  3. Front Door to the block is often always open because it's broke, once again anyone can walk in.

  4. Human waste pipe leaking in main entrance (where the letter boxes are). This leaves the foyer smelling like a sewer.

  5. Very big problem with rats and foxes in the bin shed due to previous issue stated and also bin shed has two doors, one is missing allowing rats and foxes entry to the bin shed and no permanent pest control. Only as and when visits.

  6. Communal area's are not always clean due to lack of appropiate cleaners.

  7. There are two lifts in the block, one is almost always waiting for repair and it's not uncommon for both lifts to be broke at the same time. Carrying bags of shopping. babies, prams, bicycles, work items up and down a potential 16 flights of stairs.

  8. The video intercom does not work for many residents, so if you have visitors you may have to go downstairs to let them in. Providing issue 3 has been fixed.

You have been warned!
